  • Exclusions: No other services, offer creation, or strategic consulting are included during this phase.
Phase 2: Month 2 Onwards (Expansion)

  • Continued Email Outreach: Ongoing management of cold email campaigns for website development.
  • YouTube Channel Guidance: Assignment of a dedicated team member to provide guidance on your YouTube channel (content strategy, topic selection, and performance review).

This future phase will include offer creation, sales script optimization, and deeper strategic consulting. This transition is not automatic and will be discussed based on performance data.
